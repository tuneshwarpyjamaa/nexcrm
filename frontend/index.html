Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions from teams evaluating NexCRM.

Is NexCRM good for non-technical teams?
Yes. The UI is intentionally minimal, with clear navigation and predictable actions that match day-to-day sales workflows.
Can we migrate our data?
You can export your data and restore it to a new workspace. If you’re moving from spreadsheets, import/export workflows keep it simple.
What happens if we stop paying?
You can cancel anytime. You retain access according to your plan, and you can export your data before changing plans.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. The layout is responsive and designed to work smoothly on phone, tablet, and desktop.
